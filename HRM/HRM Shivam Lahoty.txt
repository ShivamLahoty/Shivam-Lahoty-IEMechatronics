"The Pursuit of Happyness" teaches a powerful lesson on resilience and determination. The film chronicles Chris Gardner's inspiring journey from homelessness to a successful career. Amidst financial struggles and hardships, Gardner's unwavering commitment to his dreams, paired with his dedication as a father, becomes a testament to the human spirit's strength. The movie underscores the importance of perseverance, hard work, and maintaining hope in the face of adversity. It motivates audiences to overcome challenges, pursue their goals relentlessly, and believe that with resilience, even the most difficult circumstances can be transformed into opportunities for personal and professional success.

-Shivam Lahoty
